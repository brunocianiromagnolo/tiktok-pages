Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ReelPilot. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application to schedule and publish content to TikTok and Instagram.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 TikTok Integration

We utilize the official TikTok API Services. We collect and process:

  • Account Information: Your email address and basic profile information for authentication.
  • TikTok OAuth Tokens: Secure tokens granted by TikTok to allow us to publish on your behalf. We do not store your TikTok password.
  • User Content: Video files and captions you upload to ReelPilot for scheduling.
  • Usage Data: Basic logs (IP address, browser type) to monitor service security.

2.2 Instagram Integration

We utilize the official Instagram Content Publishing API and Meta Graph API. We collect and process:

  • Instagram Business Account Information: Your Instagram username, profile picture, and account ID for authentication and display in your dashboard.
  • Facebook Page Access: Access to Facebook Pages you manage, required to identify which Instagram Business Accounts are connected to your Pages (Instagram API requires linking to a Facebook Page).
  • Meta OAuth Tokens: Secure access tokens granted by Meta to allow us to publish Reels on your behalf. We do not store your Facebook or Instagram password.
  • Video Content: Reels you upload to schedule for publication to Instagram.
  • Analytics Data: Basic engagement metrics (views, likes) for published posts, if you enable this feature.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and maintain your session.
  • Core Functionality: To schedule and automatically publish videos to your TikTok and Instagram accounts at your specified times.
  • Dashboard Display: To show your connected accounts, scheduled posts, and publishing history.
  • Analytics (Optional): To display performance metrics for your published content.
  • Security: To monitor and protect the integrity of our service.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data in the following circumstances:

  • With TikTok: To fulfill your request to publish content to TikTok.
  • With Meta/Instagram: To fulfill your request to publish content to Instagram.
  • Service Providers: We may use trusted third-party cloud providers (e.g., Supabase for database hosting) who are bound by confidentiality agreements. These providers only process data on our behalf and do not have independent access.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing the ReelPilot service.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the service:

  • OAuth Tokens: Stored securely until you disconnect your account.
  • Scheduled Posts: Retained until published, then moved to historical records.
  • Historical Records: Kept for 90 days after publication, then deleted.

5.2 How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Disconnect Accounts

  • TikTok: Revoke ReelPilot's access via your TikTok Security Settings.
  • Instagram: Revoke access via Facebook Business Integrations.
  • In ReelPilot: Go to Settings → Connected Accounts → Disconnect

Option 2: Full Account Deletion

Email us at: reelpilot.contact@gmail.com

Subject: "Data Deletion Request"

Include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Accounts you wish to delete (TikTok, Instagram, or both)

We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted:

  • Your account credentials
  • All OAuth access tokens
  • Scheduled and historical posts
  • Uploaded videos

What Remains (Anonymized):

  • Aggregated analytics with no personal identifiers

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256.
  • HTTPS: All data transmission uses secure HTTPS connections.
  • Access Control: Limited access to production data, restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular Audits: We regularly review our security practices.

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (see Section 5.2).
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at reelpilot.contact@gmail.com.
